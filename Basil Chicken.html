Spicy Thai Basil Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground chicken
  • 1 cup Thai basil leaves
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 red chili peppers, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, fish sauce, oyster sauce, and sugar. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and sliced chili peppers to the pan. Saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the ground chicken to the pan and cook until it's no longer pink, breaking it up into small crumbles.
  5. Pour the sauce mixture over the chicken and stir well to coat.
  6. Continue cooking for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  7. Remove the pan from heat and stir in the Thai basil leaves. They will wilt from the residual heat.
  8. Serve the Spicy Thai Basil Chicken over steamed rice and enjoy!
